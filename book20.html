Sapiens

Yuval Noah Harari

Explores the history, evolution, and future of humankind.
Sapiens explores the history of humankind from early evolution to modern civilization. Yuval Noah Harari examines how biology, culture, religion, and technology shaped human societies. The book explains how shared myths, beliefs, and systems allowed humans to cooperate on a large scale. Harari discusses major revolutions, including the Cognitive Revolution, Agricultural Revolution, and Scientific Revolution. He questions common assumptions about progress, happiness, and human dominance. The book is thought-provoking and encourages readers to critically analyze humanity’s past and future. Written in an engaging and accessible style, Sapiens blends history, science, and philosophy. It is ideal for readers interested in understanding how humans became what they are today and where civilization might be heading.
