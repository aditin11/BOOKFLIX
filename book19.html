The Intelligent Investor

Benjamin Graham

A foundation book on value investing and long-term strategy.
The Intelligent Investor is a foundational book on value investing. Benjamin Graham introduces the concept of investing with discipline, patience, and long-term perspective. The book emphasizes analyzing companies based on fundamentals rather than market trends or emotions. Graham introduces key ideas such as “margin of safety” and differentiates between investing and speculation. He advises investors to manage risk carefully and avoid impulsive decisions driven by market fluctuations. Although the book is technical at times, its core principles are timeless. It teaches readers how to think rationally about investments and build sustainable wealth. The Intelligent Investor is especially valuable for serious investors and finance students. It promotes emotional control, critical thinking, and long-term financial stability.
