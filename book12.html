Shoe Dog

Phil Knight

The inspiring journey behind the creation of Nike.
Shoe Dog is the memoir of Phil Knight, the founder of Nike. The book tells the honest story behind building one of the world’s most successful brands. Knight shares the challenges, failures, financial struggles, and risks he faced while growing Nike from a small startup into a global company. Rather than presenting success as easy, the book highlights uncertainty, self-doubt, and perseverance. Knight discusses the importance of trusting the journey, building a strong team, and staying committed despite setbacks. The book offers insight into entrepreneurship, innovation, and leadership. Shoe Dog is engaging and inspirational because it shows the human side of success. It teaches that passion and persistence matter more than immediate profits. The book is ideal for students, entrepreneurs, and anyone interested in business, as it reveals the reality behind building something meaningful from scratch.
