Think Like a Monk

Jay Shetty

Explores mindfulness, discipline, and purpose using ancient monk wisdom.
Think Like a Monk blends ancient wisdom with modern self-improvement techniques. Jay Shetty draws on his experience as a monk to explain how we can reduce stress, improve focus, and find purpose in everyday life. The book teaches readers how to detach from negativity and build a life aligned with their values. The book is divided into three sections: letting go, growing, and giving. It emphasizes discipline, gratitude, mindfulness, and service to others. Through practical exercises and relatable stories, Shetty explains how monk principles can be applied in careers, relationships, and personal growth. Think Like a Monk is not about renouncing the world but about changing the way we think and respond to challenges. It encourages self-awareness, emotional control, and clarity. The book is especially helpful for readers seeking mental peace and balance in a fast-paced world. Its conversational tone makes deep concepts easy to understand and implement.
