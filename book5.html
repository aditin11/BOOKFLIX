Ikigai

Hector Garcia & Francesc Miralles

Introduces the Japanese philosophy of finding meaning and happiness in everyday life.
Ikigai explores the Japanese concept of finding purpose and joy in life. The word “Ikigai” refers to the reason one wakes up every morning—a combination of passion, mission, profession, and vocation. The book studies the lifestyles of long-living people in Okinawa, Japan, to uncover the secrets of happiness and longevity. The authors emphasize living a balanced life, staying active, maintaining strong social connections, and doing meaningful work. Instead of chasing success aggressively, Ikigai promotes consistency, patience, and mindfulness. The book combines philosophy, psychology, and cultural insights in a simple and calming manner. It encourages readers to slow down, appreciate small joys, and align daily activities with long-term purpose. Ikigai is ideal for those feeling lost or burned out, offering gentle guidance toward a more fulfilling and peaceful life.
