Start With Why

Simon Sinek

Explains how leaders inspire action by focusing on purpose first.
Start With Why explains how great leaders inspire action by focusing on purpose rather than profit. Simon Sinek introduces the “Golden Circle” model: Why, How, and What. The book argues that people connect emotionally with purpose-driven organizations. By clearly defining why they exist, leaders can motivate teams and build trust. The book is widely used in leadership, marketing, and entrepreneurship. It encourages clarity, vision, and authenticity. Start With Why is ideal for those aspiring to lead or build meaningful organizations.
